Seasonal Trips

Home Seasonal Trips

Captain Dylan targets the rivers and oceans on the Central Oregon Coast. During the fall and winter we target salmon and steelhead on the Alsea and Siletz River. Unlike other guides who trailer long distances to fish these locations Dylan lives in Philomath, Oregon and fish the rivers with regularity. During the spring and summer we fish the North and South Santiam Rivers and the McKenzie River. On these rivers we target Spring Chinook, Summer Steelhead, cutthroat trout and redside trout.

We also target salmon, rockfish, lingcod and crab in the Pacific Ocean. We work with other reputable guides in the region to get clients out on the ocean when the weather allows.

Our trip rates are listed for two anglers. On most trips two anglers is the maximum Captain Dylan will take down the river to ensure adequate fishing room in the drift boat.
